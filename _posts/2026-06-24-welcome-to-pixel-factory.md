This site is for the things that do not fit neatly anywhere else.

It is for small obsessions, things worth learning properly, references I want to keep, and thoughts that would otherwise disappear into drafts, tabs, or scattered notes.

<!--more-->

The idea is simple: if something feels worth revisiting, it gets written down here in plain text.

That means some posts will be polished and some will be compact. The important thing is that they stay legible, searchable, and easy to return to later.
