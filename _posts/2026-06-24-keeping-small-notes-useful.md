---
title: Keeping small notes useful
date: 2026-06-24
tags: [writing, learning]
description: A starter thought on making short notes readable enough to be worth keeping.
---
The problem with a lot of quick notes is not that they are short. It is that they are vague.

A useful short note usually does at least one concrete thing:

- names the idea clearly
- says why it mattered
- leaves a keyword or phrase you can search for later

That is enough to turn a passing thought into something you can actually find again.
